About Us

Gujarat Embroidery is a platform dedicated to preserving and promoting the vibrant textile arts of Gujarat. We collaborate with skilled women artisans from rural communities who create intricate patterns in traditional styles like Kutch, Rabari, and Mirror work. Our mission is to bring their craft to the global stage, support sustainable fashion, and empower artisan families through fair trade.

Through this initiative, we aim to raise awareness about the cultural significance of embroidery in Gujarat and encourage appreciation for handmade textiles. Every thread tells a story — of tradition, identity, and resilience. Our platform also offers products for conscious consumers looking to make a difference through their choices.
